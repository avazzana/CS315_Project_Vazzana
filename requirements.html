Time commitments of active members

Each member is required to attend a chapter meeting every Thursday night at 7pm. Chapter meetings can last up to an hour an a half, but most are shorter! Additionally, each member must serve on at least one committee. Most committees meet once per week and have meetings that last around half an hour. In addition, we have a few other requirements throughout the year:

Requirements for Active Members

Category Requirement
Sisterhood 2 credits per semester
Philanthropy 2 credits per semester
Fundraising One shift at each event
Recruitment 2 events per semester, plus formal recruitment

In addition, each year members are required to give a performance, serve at a student recital, and attend one executive meeting.
